# User Personas

## Persona: Astro Employee

### Overview
The primary user of the Easy App mobile application. Represents all Astro employees who need to access company services, manage their profile, submit claims, make payments, and stay informed about company announcements.

### Demographics
- **Role**: Astro employee (various departments and positions)
- **Age Range**: 25-55 years
- **Technical Proficiency**: Moderate to high (comfortable with mobile apps)
- **Device Usage**: Primarily uses smartphone for work-related tasks

### Goals
- Access company services and information quickly and easily
- Manage personal profile and account information
- Submit expense claims and track their status
- Make payments using QR wallet functionality
- Stay updated with company announcements and news
- Participate in company wellness programs (Steps Challenge)
- Get support when needed (AstroDesk)
- Report issues or concerns (Report Piracy)

### Pain Points
- Need to access multiple systems for different tasks
- Difficulty tracking claim status and payment history
- Missing important company announcements
- Complex authentication processes
- Limited offline access to important information
- Difficulty navigating between different company services

### Motivations
- Efficiency: Complete tasks quickly without switching between multiple apps
- Convenience: Access all company services from one mobile app
- Transparency: Track claims, payments, and announcements in real-time
- Engagement: Participate in company programs and stay connected
- Support: Easy access to help and support when needed

### Technology Context
- Uses corporate Astro email account for authentication
- Has access to company network and services
- May use both Android and iOS devices
- Expects secure, fast, and reliable mobile experience
- Values offline access for viewing cached content

### Usage Patterns
- **Frequency**: Daily to weekly usage
- **Primary Use Cases**: 
  - Check announcements and company news
  - Submit and track expense claims
  - Make payments via QR wallet
  - Update profile information
  - Participate in Steps Challenge
  - Access support services
- **Peak Usage**: During work hours, lunch breaks, and commute times

### Expectations
- Fast and responsive app performance
- Secure authentication and data protection
- Intuitive navigation and user interface
- Reliable offline viewing capabilities
- Clear error messages and helpful guidance
- Consistent experience across Android, iOS, and Web platforms

### Story Mappings
This persona is mapped to all user stories in the Easy App, as all features are designed for Astro employees. The persona's goals, pain points, and motivations inform the design and acceptance criteria of all stories.
